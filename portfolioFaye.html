About me

Hello, I'm Faye! I'm a Creative Technology student at TU Twente and Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam. I'm passionate about design, art, and (digital) creativity. Creativity has always been a big part of my life. I'm creative in different ways: I love 3D modeling, Digital Art, painting, design, technical projects, and I also enjoy fashion. I work as a model, which allows me to travel a lot, something I really love. Being in different places and surrounded by diverse styles and cultures constantly inspires me. I started traveling solo at 17, some of the places I've visited include Japan, England, France, Thailand, Vietnam and Italy.
